Our Story

Built On Trust & Steel

SteelRise Construction was founded in 2000 in Ludhiana, Punjab by a team of passionate engineers with one vision — to build structures that stand the test of time.

What started as a small team of 10 dedicated professionals has grown into Punjab's most trusted construction company with over 120 skilled team members, engineers, architects, and project managers.

Over 25 years, we've delivered 850+ projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors — from luxury villas in Chandigarh to industrial plants in Ludhiana.

Our commitment is simple: quality over everything, delivered on time and within budget.

Our Journey

25 Years of Excellence

2000
The Beginning
Founded in Ludhiana by Harjinder Singh with a team of 10 engineers and a dream to build Punjab's finest structures.
2005
First Major Commercial Project
Completed our first large-scale commercial complex in Ludhiana — a 5-storey office building that put SteelRise on the map.
2010
Expansion to Chandigarh
Opened our second office in Chandigarh and took on our first government infrastructure contract worth ₹50 crore.
2015
100+ Projects Milestone
Crossed 100 completed projects and received the Punjab Excellence in Construction Award.
2020
Going Green
Adopted sustainable construction practices and achieved ISO 14001 environmental certification.
2025
850+ Projects & Growing
Today we stand with 850+ completed projects, 120+ team members, and a vision to build across all of North India.
